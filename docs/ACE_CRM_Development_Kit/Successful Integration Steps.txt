To Successfully implement ACE Partner CRM integration, please follow below Successful Integration Steps.

1. Read through ACE CRM - Get Started.pptx to get high level idea on the integration. 
2. Read through the User guide and FAQs
3. Understand file formats and field definitions.
4. Develop your Field Mapping and document Business Process Changes
5. Read through development task checklist which will provide list of tasks that a developer has to build.
6. Sample code and sample results will provide further guidance.
7. Watch videos to perform some of the steps or get to know the details
